Eydis Synthesis 10

Eydis/Edith (イーディス, Eidisu) also known as Eydis Synthesis Ten is a reoccurring character throughout the Sword Art Online series and is a supporting character in the Alicization Arc, a nd is an Integrity Knight and a close friend of Alice.

Background

Eydis used to live a normal life on a village with her younger sister Mary long time ago before Kirito dived into Underworld. While having her time with Mary, she enjoyed bathing and taking care of her. She also enjoyed telling stories with Mary before going to sleep with her.

Alice Synthesis 30

Alice Zuberg (アリス・ツーベルク, Arisu Tsūberuku) is one of the main characters in the Alicization Arc and a supporting character in the Unital Ring Arc. She is the daughter of Rulid Village's chief Gasupht Zuberg, as well as the childhood friend of Eugeo and Kirito.

Background

Alice is a girl from Rulid Village who was friends with Kirito and Eugeo until she was taken away by an Integrity Knight for touching the gravel from the Land of Darkness. As Kirito and Eugeo later find out, she was synthesized into an Integrity Knight herself.
